🆕 Effective July 1, 2024

India's New Criminal Laws

Three codes replaced 160-year-old colonial laws on July 1, 2024. If your case was filed after that date, these laws apply to you — not the old IPC/CrPC.

Replaced IPC 1860
Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita
BNS · Act 45 of 2023

Defines what is a crime. All criminal offences — cruelty, fraud, cheating — are now defined here.

Key changes for men:
  • 📌 Old 498A → Now § 85 BNS
  • 📌 Old 406 → Now § 316 BNS
  • 📌 Old 182 → Now § 216 BNS
  • 📌 Old 211 → Now § 250 BNS
  • 📌 Old 499/500 → Now § 356/357 BNS
Replaced CrPC 1973
Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita
BNSS · Act 46 of 2023

Defines how crimes are investigated and how bail, FIR, and trials work.

Key changes for men:
  • 📌 Old Anticipatory Bail 438 → § 482 BNSS
  • 📌 Old FIR Quashing 482 → § 528 BNSS
  • 📌 Old Maintenance 125 → § 144 BNSS
  • 📌 Old Police Notice 41A → § 35 BNSS
  • 📌 Police custody capped at 15 days
Replaced Evidence Act 1872
Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam
BSA · Act 47 of 2023

Defines what counts as evidence. WhatsApp, emails, call recordings are now PRIMARY evidence.

Key changes for men:
  • 📌 § 63 BSA — WhatsApp chats ✅ primary
  • 📌 Call recordings ✅ primary
  • 📌 Emails & SMS ✅ primary
  • 📌 CCTV / location data ✅ primary
  • 📌 Certificate needed under § 63(4) BSA
⚠️
Which law applies to your case?
  • ✅ Filed before July 1, 2024 → IPC + CrPC + Indian Evidence Act
  • ✅ Filed on or after July 1, 2024 → BNS + BNSS + BSA
  • ✅ Section numbers changed — substance of law is mostly the same

🆘 Emergency Legal Help

⚠️
If you have been arrested or are about to be: Do NOT sign anything. Ask for a lawyer immediately. You have the right to remain silent.
1
Stay calm — do NOT argue with police

Say: "I want to speak with my lawyer before answering any questions."

2
Call NALSA for free legal aid

Dial 15100 — free legal aid is your right under Article 22.

3
Apply for Anticipatory Bail (BNSS § 482)

File before arrest at Sessions Court. A lawyer can do this immediately.

4
Document everything

Write down names, times, events. Screenshot everything. Witnesses matter.
