India's New Criminal Laws

BNS replaced IPC. BNSS replaced CrPC. BSA replaced the Evidence Act. If your case was filed after July 1, 2024, these laws apply.

Filed before July 1, 2024? → IPC + CrPC + Evidence Act apply.
Filed on/after July 1, 2024? → BNS + BNSS + BSA apply.

🆘 If You Are Being Arrested

⚠
Stay calm. Do NOT argue, resist, or sign anything.
1
Say nothing without a lawyer

"I want to speak with my lawyer before answering any questions."

2
Call NALSA for a free lawyer

Dial 15100 — free legal aid is your constitutional right (Article 22).

3
Apply for Anticipatory Bail (BNSS § 482)

File before arrest at Sessions Court. Can be done the same day.

4
Write everything down

Names, times, what was said. Every detail matters for your case.
